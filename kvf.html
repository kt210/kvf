About Us

Kusum Vatsalya Foundation is a voice for women, a shield for the vulnerable, and a path to a better future. We offer legal aid, emotional support, and community programs to build confidence, safety, and skills.

Founded with love and courage, we stand for change, unity, and women's rights across all walks of life.
